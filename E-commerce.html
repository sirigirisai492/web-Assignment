FAQs

What are GTG perfumes made of? +

Our perfumes are crafted with a blend of premium natural essential oils and high-quality synthetic aroma molecules, ensuring both longevity and a rich, complex scent profile. We prioritize skin safety and sustainability in our ingredient sourcing.

How long do GTG fragrances last? +

GTG fragrances are formulated for exceptional longevity, typically lasting 10-12 hours on the skin. The exact duration can vary based on skin type, climate, and the specific fragrance composition.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping! Shipping costs and delivery times will vary depending on your location. You can find more details on our shipping policy page.
